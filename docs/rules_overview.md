# Rules Overview (DnD-Inspired)

This system is fully inspired by DnD 5e style play, adapted for a space opera setting.

## Core resolution
- Roll a d20 + modifier vs DC or target AC.
- Advantage/Disadvantage: roll 2d20, take higher/lower.
- Natural 20 = critical success; natural 1 = critical failure (table optional).

## Ability scores
- STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA.
- Modifiers are the same as DnD: (score - 10) / 2 rounded down.

## Proficiency
- Proficiency bonus scales by level (1-20).
- Applies to trained skills, saving throws, and weapon/armor use.

## Leveling
- Level range: 1-20.
- Hit points: class base + CON modifier per level.
- Feats or ability score increases at standard DnD-style tiers.

## Combat structure
- Initiative: d20 + DEX.
- Turn: action, bonus action, movement, reaction.
- Armor Class (AC) and cover rules mirror DnD, with tech armor variants.

## Damage and conditions
- Use standard DnD damage types plus Sci-Fi types:
  - Energy (laser), Plasma, Ion, Kinetic, Explosive, Radiation.
- Conditions mirror DnD with added: Overheated, Ionized, Zero-G.

## Resting and recovery
- Short rest: 1 hour.
- Long rest: 8 hours with safe environment.

## Tech powers
- Use DnD spell slot structure, renamed as Tech/Psi power slots.
- Classes gain access to Tech or Psi disciplines.

## Space and vehicles
- Ship combat uses the same action economy with ship roles.
- Ships have AC, HP, shields, and system slots.
