# Weapons and Gear (Futuristic)

Weapons follow DnD-style properties: Light, Finesse, Heavy, Reach, Two-Handed, Loading, Ammunition, and Special.

## Weapon categories
- Energy: laser pistol, laser rifle, plasma carbine, ion blaster.
- Kinetic: mag pistol, gauss rifle, rail marksman rifle.
- Melee: vibroblade, monoblade, shock baton, energy lance.
- Heavy: rotary cannon, missile launcher, particle beam.
- Thrown/Explosive: frag grenade, EMP grenade, thermite charge.

## Example properties
- Laser pistol: light, finesse, energy damage.
- Plasma carbine: two-handed, energy/plasma damage, overheats.
- Rail marksman rifle: heavy, two-handed, long range.
- Vibroblade: light, finesse, melee.
- Energy lance: reach, two-handed, energy melee.

## Armor and shields
- Armor mirrors DnD tiers: light, medium, heavy.
- Shield generators provide bonus AC or temporary shields.

## Gear tags
- Tech: interacts with hacking/ion effects.
- Power: requires battery or reactor.
- Moddable: can accept upgrades or perks.
